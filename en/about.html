Julia

In my work, safety, warmth and a caring human presence matter most to me.

I create a space where you can be yourself, speak honestly and gradually hear your bodily sensations, thoughts, feelings and inner compass more clearly.

It is important to me to keep this sense of respect, attention and care in every interaction with a client.

Professional experience

10+ years in management

Before coaching, I spent many years in business areas connected with premium products and services; my experience of leading people spans more than ten years.

This path taught me to listen to a person attentively, to notice what makes them unique and to find an approach based not on templates, but on respect, attention and a living connection.

My path

The wish to help people has accompanied me at different stages of my life. It has always given me a sense of warmth, meaning and inner fullness.

Before coaching, I worked for many years in marketing, brand management and business development. This experience taught me to see people more deeply — their values, inner processes and ways of relating to the world.

Over time I understood ever more clearly that I wanted to return to what truly matters to me — accompanying people on their inner journey.

It was important for me to go through personal therapy and to rethink the role of the Rescuer.

Today I choose to help not out of a wish to fill inner gaps, but from conscious presence, respect for the person and faith in their inner resources.

What I believe

I believe that every person is unique and many-sided. It is this very uniqueness that makes the world deeper, more alive and more beautiful.

To me, a person is a value: a space of strength, feelings, talents and vulnerability that can express themselves freely and honestly.

I believe that real change begins when we stop living only by expectations, fears and others' ideas of who we are — and start to see honestly what is truly happening within us.

I am drawn to calm, deep conversations about things that matter — the kind that often hold clarity, inner meaning and the possibility of moving along your own path.

It matters to me that, beside me, a person can relax, trust and be themselves — both in their strength and in their vulnerability.

my interests

I love people, music, books, silence and life in all its expressions.

I am drawn to sincerity, depth, inner discoveries and moments of genuine contact — with myself, with others and with life.
